kew themes pack 2.

Included in this pack:

alubmcolors.theme - This is a special theme that decides how the album cover colors are used in album color mode. 4 available colors.
onealbumcolor.theme - This is a special theme that emulates the old album color mode that was based on one color.

To apply, run:

kew theme <theme>

Or cycle by pressing 't'.

These should be available under /themes here, in case you need to restore them to defaults:
