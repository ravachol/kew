1. Download the data from Wikipedia:

https://dumps.wikimedia.org/wikidatawiki/entities/latest-all.json.bz2

python3 extract_from_wikidata.py latest-all.json.bz2

2. Wash the data:

python3 keep_only_matching_urls.py artists_raw.tsv artists_matched.tsv

python3 apply_corrections.py artists_matched.tsv corrections.tsv artists_unverified.tsv

python3 verify_urls.py artists_unverified.tsv

Be aware the last step takes many hours.

3. Create the artists.db binary:

python3 make_binary_db.py artists.tsv artists.db
